Preamble: Reason for the Creation of the Government We, the founding Senators have decided that to create a more perfect clan, we must establish a written constitution. Our goals are in the interest of maintaining national and individual sovereignty. We the Senators of the Kero Clan, in order to form a more perfect clan, establish fair play, promote Friendly Battles, do protect and establish this Constitution for the Republic of Kero.

Article I: Definition of the government Section 1: Republic of Kero’s tricameral government consists of three branches. Section 2: The legislative branch shall be call the Senate. The role of this branch is to make laws and propositions, to be followed by Kero and Kayro Clans. Section 3: Supreme Court justices make up a second branch. They will discuss if a law or proposition is Kero-consitutional. Section 4: The third and final branch is called executive, which consists of only one member- the leader.

Article II: Formation of the Senate Section 1: The Senate must have a minimum of 6 members. In the case of an absence of senators, the Senate will be allowed to operate under 5 members, but no propositions or amendments shall be passed before a new member is chosen. The role of the Senators is to lead the clan, and make decisions. Section 2: If a Senator proposes an idea, the other fellow Senators will vote on it. A ? vote yes passes the amendment or proposition, making it effective. A below ? vote yes will prevent the amendment or proposition.

Article III: Interaction Section 1: People should interact with each other in a kind, friendly matter. Inappropriate comments and cussing shall result in a removal from either Kero or Kayro Clans. Section 2: When engaging in friendly battles, play fairly and use good manners, saying “Good game!”, “Good luck!” and “Well played!!” is great, but spamming the laughing or mad symbols reflect bad sportsmanship, and we want to support a friendly environment. Additionally, these requirements span into real world, where punishments can still be given.

Article IV: Amending Section 1: Amending the Constitution is allowed. Amendments are suggested by members, elders, co-leaders, or the Senate of Kero Clan, and are revised by the Senate of Kero Clan. Once the revision is finished, the Senate has a vote, and a vote of at least ? will make the amendment effective. Section 2: Senatorial votes will remain strictly confidential, and discussion of the voting will result in removal from the Senate, if occurring twice. Section 3: Bribing Senators to vote for what you think will not be tolerated. Section 4: Once votes have been made, no changes will be allowed. Section 5: The polls happen on the app Choice, in the group Republic of Kero Clan.

Article V: Voting Section 1: As mentioned in article IV, voting requires a ? vote. Each senator counts for one vote. No senator will ever have a higher counting vote than another, and the majority goes by popular vote.

Article VI: Creation of the Supreme Court. Section 1: A Supreme Court shall be created in which there are 3 justices, including a Chief Justice. Section 2: In the event of a disagreement within the constitution, they will determine if the law is Kero-constitutional or not. Determining this requires a majority vote from the Supreme Court justices. With a vote yes of a majority, the decision will be accepted, but a less than ½ vote yes will deny the decision.

Article VII: Approval of Senate Section 1: No Senator shall make a decision without approval from the Senate. Before making a decision, the Senator must discuss with other Senators by proposing their idea then letting other Senators edit. Once all the editing is finished, the vote will occur. Section 2: If a Senator makes a decision without approval, they are banned from voting for 3 days. An occurrence of this twice will result in removal from Senate.

Article VIII: Kayro Clan Section 1: Kayro Clan shall serve as a feeder for Kero Clan. Kayro Clan is the place to go to if required trophies are not met or if Kero Clan is full. Section 2: When Kero Clan increases their trophy limit, Kayro Clan shall increase their trophy limit in proportion to Kero Clan’s increase. Section 3: In an event where Kayro Clan is full, another clan shall be made.
