Can I Join?

Yes, we encourage new members to join our commmunity! Request to join and we will promptly respond.

What are the requirements to join?

Besides our current trophy minimum of 2600, we expect players to be active and donate regularly.

How can I get promoted?

Elder is earned with donations and being active, but you can earn Co-Leader by being loyal and trustworthy.

Is there a feeder clan?

Yes, check out our page on the Kayro Clan!

How can people be demoted?

You can get demoted if you stop being active or abuse your rank abilities like kicking someone without permission. Also, we have a strict rule for no cussing, it is bad manners and you will be demoted to member if you do more than twice.

How often should I play to stay in the clan?

Well, it is not required for you to be too active, but as long as you have at least 20 donations per week you will be okay. Every Saturday, people with under 20 donations will be kicked due to inactivity.

What is the Kero Clan government?

The Kero Clan government is the way of keeping Kero Clan up to date on issues inside of the clan, solving problems, and making changes.

What is the Kero Clan Constitution?

The Constitution is a written document on rules of Kero Clan and how the system works, based off of ideas from the U.S. Constitution.

Who wrote the Constitution?

The Constitution was written by two very loyal, active, and kind members of Kero Clan, also the two people who made this website- Lee and Vikram7=Rice.
