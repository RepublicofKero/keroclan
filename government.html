About the Kero Clan Government

The Kero Clan Government consists of the Senate, which makes decisions for the clan, and are also the leaders of the clan. Members of the Senate propose changes, or amendments, to the clan. A 2/3 vote yes will pass the amendment and the change will be effective, but if not everything will stay the same. Propositions are changes that deal with acceptance into Kero/Kayro Clans and the Senate.

Senators:

The current senators are ViolinL, Legends, Vikram7=Rice, YellowHamster, Kclark, Maxzenzie, GoneRogue, and 1psaonly.

Amendments:

Amendment 1: Section 1: Everyone who has not reached Frozen Peak and doesn't donate at least 100 weekly will be kicked and invited to join Kayro Clan. Section 2: People who have donated 200+ will be exempt. Section 3: ViolinL is exempt. Section 4: Vikram7=Rice must remove one of the multiple accounts.

Amendment 2: No person shall have a role of both supreme court justice and Senator.

Amendment 3: No person will be allowed to have two personal accounts in Kero Clan, but multiple accounts in Kayro Clan is permitted.

Amendment 4: In an event where Kero Clan is full, the lowest person will be kicked and sent to Kayro Clan. Co-leaders are exempt.

Amendment 5: If a person kicks another member without approval from at least two members of the Senate and one member of the Supreme Court, will be kicked, but permitted to join Kayro Clan.
