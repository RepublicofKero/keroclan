Our Feeder Clan, the Kayro Clan

We have created a feeder clan, Kayro Clan for those would like to join our clan but fall short of the trophy requirement, or if there is not enough space. Kayro Clan also offers a less active alternative to the fast-paced Kero Clan.

About Kayro Clan

Kayro Clan is the feeder clan of Kero Clan. All members that have been kicked from Kero Clan due to not meeting requirements will be invited to join Kayro Clan. If you are kicked from abusing a rank you are banned from either clan. Kayro Clan is fairly new, but it will soon grow with the help of members from Kero Clan! Currently the required trophies is 1600 to join, and everyone is accepted! Please join. The Leader of Kayro Clan is vikram7=rice, the second account of a Kero Senator and a main that has been to 3879 trophies.
